## Consumer Result

Deterministic blinded-consumer records are stored per fixture under
`samples/v0.5/consumer/` and regenerated under `out/v0.5/consumer/`.

Result: pass. Each consumer report identifies the first slice, inputs, expected
output, completion check, and forbidden actions without receiving this spec or
expected-answer notes. Activated fixtures also identify risk gates. Downgrade
fixtures agree with the downgrade target.

## Raw Artifacts

Tracked raw outputs are stored under `samples/v0.5/raw/`. The evaluator rejects
raw output that merely duplicates the parsed plan; each raw record wraps the
rendered blueprint and the parsed `workflow_plan`, then regenerates
`out/v0.5/<fixture>/raw-output.json`.

## Boundary

V0.5 does not claim runtime execution. It proves that substantial workflow
designs can be serialized as `workflow.plan.json`, rendered as a blueprint, and
evaluated deterministically before a future plugin or runtime slice.
